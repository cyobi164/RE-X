What is OtaGeki

Otagei (ヲタ芸) refers to a dance style that emerged around 2009, utilizing glow sticks called "cyalumes." It has gained popularity
overseas as 'Cyalume Dance.' Otagei involves using favorite songs or trending tracks, constructing 'moves' based on the
selected song's mood or imagery, and dancing while coordinating the colors of the glow sticks.

Additionally, enthusiasts can create their own moves, upload them on platforms like Twitter, and other teams or individuals might use them. Otagei isn't limited to Japan; it's an originating cultural dance form from Japan.
You can also create your own techniques and upload them to Twitter etc. for other teams or individuals to use. Wotagei is not a culture limited to Japan. Otaku itself is a culture that originated in Japan.

It is now gaining recognition all over the world, including the United States, China, South Korea, Taiwan, the Philippines, and Singapore.
